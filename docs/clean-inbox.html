  2. 2
    Tick the checkbox at the top-left of the email list (above the first email). This selects all emails visible on screen (usually 50).

    Click the 🗑 trash / delete icon in the toolbar. All ScoutBot emails move to Trash and are permanently deleted after 30 days (or go to Trash → click "Empty Trash" to delete immediately).
